Walter White

The man who broke bad

Here's a timeline of Walter's transformation:

  • 2008 - Diagnosed with lung cancer and starts cooking meth with Jesse
  • 2009 -Escalates his involvement in the drug world, killing rival drug dealers and becoming a major player in the Albuquerque drug scene
  • 2010 - - Starts using the pseudonym "Heisenberg" and becomes known for his blue meth, which is of superior quality to any other meth on the market
  • 2011 - Becomes involved with the Mexican drug cartel and starts a war with Gus Fring, the owner of a major drug distribution network
  • 2012 - Becomes a fugitive and goes on the run with his family
  • 2013 - Returns to Albuquerque and surrenders to the DEA, but orchestrates a complex plan to eliminate his enemies and secure his family's future before his death
  • 2014 - Walter's legacy continues to haunt those around him, particularly Jesse who is held captive by a group of neo-Nazis who force him to cook meth. Walter comes out of hiding to save Jesse and eliminate his captors, but sustains a fatal gunshot wound in the process.
  • 2015 - The aftermath of Walter's actions continues to reverberate through the lives of those he touched. His family faces scrutiny and suspicion from law enforcement, while his former business partners and enemies scramble to fill the power vacuum he left behind.

"Walter's life and actions are a testament to the power of desperation and the consequences of breaking bad. Despite his intelligence and talent, his choices led to a tragic and destructive end."
